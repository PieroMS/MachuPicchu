Popular Destinations

These are some favorite places tourists visit. People usually walk around and take many photos

Terrazas

The terraces or platforms are representative structures of the Inca Empire, were a significant advance for agriculture with which they managed to generate microclimates.

Machu Picchu

The Huayna Picchu mountain is undoubtedly the most desired and popular attraction of Machu Picchu, it is the singular mountain that is seen in all the classic photos of Machu Picchu, climbing it is an incredible adventure.

Aguas Termales

It is a small cosmopolitan town located just below Machu Picchu. It is located at 2,040 meters above sea level, is known as Machu Picchu village due to its proximity to the Inca City.

Palacio Real y Acllahuasi

The Royal Palace is a house made with fine masonry and is the largest in the place, in addition to having a preferential place. The Acllahuasi was probably a place where virgins dedicated to religiosity lived.
